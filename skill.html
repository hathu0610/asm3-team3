Skills and Jobs

1.Full-stack developer

We are looking for experienced Full Stack engineers to join our development team. In this role, you are responsible for the overall development and deployment of client and server software applications. Your responsibilities range from system architecture design to advanced programming, performance testing, and system integration.

Success as a full-stack engineer requires advanced programming skills, application development experience, and good problem-solving skills. The engineer must have proficiency in both frontend (HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, C++, and JQuery) and backend (Java, Python, Rails, Ruby, .NET, and PHP) programming languages.

As our company’s product is related to AI, advanced knowledge and understanding in using python is a plus. Furthermore, the personnel is also expected to communicate with and collaborate with the AI development team from the company. Therefore, standard communication skills are also essential.

2.AI engineer

We are in need of an AI engineer who is responsible for developing, programming and training an AI model that is able to communicate with people like a real person using training data collected from several popular messaging apps such as: Messenger (Meta), Whatsapp, Twitter, Zalo, etc. The personnel is also expected to devise simple training application models that can be used by the development team. Furthermore, there will be frequent collaboration between the AI team and the software development team. Hence, the engineer must be efficient in using the python programming language and other python AI libraries like tensorflow, keras, pytorch. Furthermore, there is also a requirement for considerate communication skills such as presentation, team collaboration.

3.Product Manager and Project Manager

We are hiring someone smart who will be in charge of all product development and strategy. The person will be responsible for the management of our company product, including but not limited to: divide product into several milestones for the sale and development teams, propose plans for future development of the product. Proficiency in trended Agile Project Management is a must. Moreover, since this position requires the management of several departments in the company, high communication skills will also be required for smooth collaboration and communication, especially when pitching ideas, plans for the investors.

4.Sales manager and Business development manager

There is an available position for a business development manager, who is capable of advertising and pitching the product’s idea for potential clients, investors. Furthermore, this person should be able to work with the product manager and set up realistic development plans, milestones for the team based on the given idea, and discussion from the team. This development plan would be then used to pitch back to the product’s owner and changed depending on the feedback from the presentation.

Hence, communication skills from this position is a must and is expected to be high. Furthermore, it would be advantageous for the personnel to get along well with the other development teams, co-workers for easier communication and collaboration. In addition, accounting skills will also be needed in this job as there will also be the responsibility of managing the company’s financial situation.
